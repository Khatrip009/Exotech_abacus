📘 how formula‑perfect generation works
  • Each of the 25 problems contains 6 numbers (10‑99) with random +/‑ .
  • The generator simulates every step on the abacus (ones and tens columns) and detects which small/big friend formula would be needed.
  • If you select a specific formula (e.g. +1), every non‑direct move in the whole problem must be exactly that formula (both hands). Direct moves are allowed.
  • For “no formula”, every step must be doable without any complement (direct beads only).
  • All intermediate results stay between 0 and 99. The generator loops until 25 valid problems are found.
