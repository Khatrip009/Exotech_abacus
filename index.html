📘 how bead‑accurate generation works
  • Each rod (tens/ones) has upper=5, lower=1..4. Real bead transitions are simulated.
  • Small friend: used when adding 1‑4 with insufficient lower beads (upper free).
  • Big friend: adding 6‑9 with carry, or subtracting 6‑9 with borrow.
  • Direct moves allowed in any problem. At least one step uses selected formula (if any).
  • Problems are built step‑by‑step, rejecting any invalid transition.
